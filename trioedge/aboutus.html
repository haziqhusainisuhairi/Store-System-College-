JPK KKA

Jawatankuasa Perwakilan Kolej, which known as JPK is an organization to organize about matters regarding colleges residents and non-residents. They are consists of students in UiTM. This organization also plans and handles events that consists of residents and students of Kolej Kediaman A (KKA) to improve the relationship between them and also to remain a place in college.

A B O U T   O U R   S Y S T E M


Our system is all about the ease of organizing the items in the store. Our team manages all the data of students and saves it into our system. Our main goals in developing the system are to establish a centralized database and have secured data since it is stored in the system and to develop a new function to store new data to prevent it from being easily destroyed. Next, it will be more environmentally friendly because we hope for a less wastage of paper and space and just need to rely on a computer. Other than that, by making work less time consuming, it can ease the process by coming up with a registration mode and the student does not need to wait in a line to wait for their turn.
